Mudi is a global community connecting shoppers and travelers. We are a social platform for shoppers seeking to buy items from around the world, and for travelers looking to make money by delivering these items.
You can subsidize your trip every time you travel. The buyer must register his order, with the product name, product`s url, image`s url and description.
The order will be set as "pending" and will appear in the "Make an offer" section for a prospective traveler. The traveler can check all the information and make an offer, which is the price plus the reward he wants for the service. If the buyer accept the condition, he must pay the amount agreed and wait until the delivered date. Upon delivery, you will be reimbursed for the price of the item and paid your reward.
